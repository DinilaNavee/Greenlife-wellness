About Us

GreenLife Wellness Center is a trusted health and lifestyle organization based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, dedicated to promoting holistic well-being and natural healing. Our center offers a wide range of personalized wellness services including Ayurvedic Therapy, Yoga and Meditation, Physiotherapy, Massage Therapy, and Nutrition and Diet Consultation. We believe in a balanced approach to health—focusing not only on physical treatments but also on mental and emotional wellness. With a team of experienced therapists and wellness consultants, GreenLife creates a nurturing environment where clients can achieve harmony in mind, body, and spirit. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, looking to improve your fitness, or simply want to enhance your overall quality of life, GreenLife Wellness Center is committed to guiding you on your journey to better health—naturally and compassionately.
